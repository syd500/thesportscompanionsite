// Strategy Tips
⚡

Speed Bonus

Answer within 5 seconds for maximum bonus points. Score = 1 + (timeLeft ÷ 15). Fast and correct beats slow and correct.

📈

Study YPPG Diff

Teams with high YPPG Diff pass more on 1st down. When in doubt, pass-heavy offenses throw — especially up by 10+.

🔥

Protect Your Streak

A long streak multiplies your leaderboard ranking impact. When unsure, pick the statistically most common play type for the situation.

🎯

Down & Distance

3rd & long = pass 78% of the time league-wide. 3rd & short = run or QB sneak. 2nd & 10+ = mix, lean pass.

// Strategy Tips
🎯

0-2 Count = Waste Pitch

On 0-2, pitchers throw out of the zone 62% of the time. Expect a breaking ball low or a fastball up and in.

📐

Study the Zone

Middle of the zone = most common location on 3-0 counts. Away = most common vs. right-handed batters with runners on.

⚡

Pick Fast

Same speed bonus as Guess the Play: score += 1 + (timeLeft ÷ 15). Correct in 3 seconds beats correct in 13.

📈

Use RPI

Low RPI pitchers (elite control) throw more strikes. Expect In Zone picks more often when facing a sub-2.50 RPI arm.

// Strategy Tips
⏱️

Think PPM, Not PPG

A player averaging 18 PPG in 28 minutes outperforms a 22 PPG player in 40 minutes on a per-minute basis. Always check PPM.

📈

Net Rating Wins Games

Teams with Net Rating +6 or better win 74% of games. Back the team with the larger positive net rating in close matchups.

🎯

TS% Over FG%

True Shooting % accounts for threes and free throws. A 55% TS player is elite. Use this over FG% for all lineup decisions.

🔥

Pace Matters

High-pace teams (100+ possessions/game) generate more fantasy points. Target players on fast-paced teams in daily lineups.
