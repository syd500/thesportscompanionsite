// Stat Definitions — from The Sports Statistics Manual by Chris Cain
OVR Soccer
Overall Rating
A 0–99 composite score combining goals, assists, xG, shots on target, and defensive contributions relative to all players in the same league.
xG Soccer
Expected Goals
The probability that a shot results in a goal based on shot location, angle, shot type, and assist type. Goals scored above xG = clinical finishing; below = poor finishing or bad luck.
xGA Soccer
Expected Goals Against
xG conceded by the defense/goalkeeper. A keeper's save percentage vs. xGA reveals true shot-stopping ability beyond what raw goals allowed shows.
SoT Soccer
Shots on Target
Shots that would have gone in without a save or hit the post. Divided by total shots to get shot accuracy.
CS Soccer
Clean Sheets
Games where the team/goalkeeper conceded zero goals. One of the primary metrics in the goalkeeper OVR calculation.
POSS% Soccer
Possession Percentage
Share of total playing time a team controls the ball. High possession + high xG = dominant team; high possession + low xG = inefficient.
