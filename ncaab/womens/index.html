// Stat Definitions — from The Sports Statistics Manual by Chris Cain
OVR NBA
Overall Rating
A 0–99 composite score based on PPM (Points Per Minute) normalized against the current college season's top and bottom performers at each position.
PPM NBA
Points Per Minute
Total points ÷ total minutes. The primary metric in the college basketball OVR system — accounts for varying playing time across starters and bench players.
PPG+ NBA
Adjusted Points Per Game
PPM × 40 minutes (college game length). Projects a player's scoring over a full college game.
KenPom All
Ken Pomeroy Rating
Adjusted efficiency margin — offensive efficiency minus defensive efficiency per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponent strength. The gold standard in college basketball analytics.
